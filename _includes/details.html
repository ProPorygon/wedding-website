Things to Do

Here are some activities in the Columbus area if you're visiting

Short North Arts District and the North Market

Plenty of shopping and food along Columbus' North High Street

German Village

Historic houses and restaurants along brick-paved streets

Scioto Mile: Olentangy and Scioto Trails

Walk or bike along the rivers on these multipurpose trails

Ohio State Campus and Stadium

Walk around the scenic university campus; Saturday is game day, so you may be able to catch a football game.

Columbus Zoo and Aquarium

Highly rated zoo, must-see animal exhibits include polar bears and manatees.

Columbus Metro Parks

Columbus has a system of metro parks, including High Banks and Blacklick Woods, which contain a number of hiking trails and nature centers.
