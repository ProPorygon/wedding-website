Retreat21

Our wedding will be held on the beautiful grounds of Retreat21, a winery located on the outskirts of Columbus, Ohio. The ceremony will be held outdoors among the trees. Dinner will be hosted indoors, and during the reception guests may spend time outdoors on the patio around the venue location.

Parking is free on-site, but it is also a quick Uber ride from nearby Dublin, Ohio.
