Kevin & Julie

September 22, 2024
Columbus, OH

Meeting in Pittsburgh

Julie and Kevin met in Pittsburgh. Julie had just come from Taiwan to study Mechanical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon Univeristy, while Kevin had just started a new job at an AI startup in the city. As Julie was busy with her studies, the two began cooking together as a way to share time efficiently. Their most memorable date was getting hot chocolate and ice skating at the holiday downtown light up night.

Long Distance

After her graduation, Julie took her first job in Houston, while Kevin remained working in Pittsburgh. The two traveled back and forth to visit each other, and quarantined together through the Covid pandemic.

Together Again in California

Following two years of long distance, the couple reunited in the sunny San Francisco Bay Area. Julie found a job in semiconductors, while Kevin was able to make the move to a new office.

The Proposal

On a clear and breezy San Francisco evening, Kevin invited Julie and her close friends on a sunset cruise. As the sun fell behind the Golden Gate Bridge, Kevin revealed a ring and proposed in Mandarin. Julie happily accepted!
